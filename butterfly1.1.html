How To Fold A Butterfly

Prepare to bring a touch of ethereal beauty to life with the art of origami! This guide will walk you through folding a delicate 3D butterfly, a symbol deeply rooted in themes of metamorphosis, graceful movement, and unfettered liberty.

This model is simple and fun to fold, making it perfect for beginners. All you need is a square piece of paper and a bit of patience.

Butterfly Step-by-Step Instructions

· Difficulty: Easy

· Folding time: 10-15 minutes

· Paper: 15*15 cm

· Total steps: 15

Step 1

Take out a square piece of paper, fold it in half vertically and horizontally.

Step 2

Now fold the paper diagonally in both directions to create creases.

Step 3

Collapse the paper inward along the creases into a triangle shape.

Step 4

Fold both corners of the triangle up to the top point.

Step 5

Flip the model over and fold the top point down past the bottom edge.

Step 6

Fold the model in half vertically (wings together).

Step 7

Bend the wings down to shape the butterfly's top wings.

Step 8

Adjust the folds gently to make the shape more 3D.

Step 9

Pull out the wings slightly to add volume.

Step 10

Gently open up the body and flatten the butterfly base.

Step 11

Shape the wings outward to create a rounded look.

Step 12

Press along the center to sharpen the fold lines.

Step 13

Finish shaping both the top and bottom wings.

Step 14

Make any final adjustments to smooth out the folds.

Step 15

Your butterfly is complete! Try different colors or sizes for fun variations!
