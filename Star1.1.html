How To Fold A Star

These instructions will show you how to make a traditional origami star. Stars are a charming and symbolic form of paper folding that are often associated with wishes, hope, and celebration.

This model is simple and fun to fold, making it perfect for beginners. All you need is a strip of paper and a bit of patience.

Star Step-by-Step Instructions

· Difficulty: Easy

· Folding time: 10-15 minutes

· Paper: 15*15 cm

· Total steps: 15 steps

Step 1

Take out a square piece of paper, fold in half horizontally.

Step 2

Fold the bottom left corner of the square up to the right edge, creating a triangle shape on the left side.

Step 3

Unfold the previous fold. Then, fold the top left corner of the square down to meet the right edge.

Step 4

Unfold the previous fold. Then, fold the bottom right corner up to meet the center of the cross.

Step 5

Fold the bottom left corner over to the right side.

Step 6

Fold in half and cut along the diagonal.

Step 7

Open it up.

Step 8

Collapse along the folds into a star base shape.

Step 9

Adjust the flaps to make the points sharper.

Step 10

Flip over and squash fold the top corner.

Step 11

Repeat the squash fold on each side.

Step 12

Begin shaping the star points by folding the outer edges inward.

Step 13

Flip it over, and tuck the flaps inside to lock the shape.

Step 14

Flatten and adjust all points symmetrically.

Step 15

Your star is complete! Decorate or use it as a gift!
